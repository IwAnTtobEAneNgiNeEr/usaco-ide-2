# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Competitive programming students, particularly Vietnamese students preparing for USACO or specialised informatics exams (HSG, Chuyên Tin), who need a fast, local-first C++ IDE and judge to practice.

## Product Purpose
Provide a local-first, fast feedback loop IDE and judge that runs entirely on the user's machine, including compilation, judging, stress-testing, and SVG input visualization, with an AI Coach to help guide solving problems without revealing direct solutions.

## Brand Personality
Expert, local-first, helpful, game-like informatics companion.

## Anti-references
Cluttered online judges (Codeforces UI, SJ/DMOJ), generic SaaS web-creams, and standard AI assistants that immediately give away code solutions.

## Design Principles
- Local-First speed: Everything must feel instantaneous.
- Non-spoiler guidance: AI Coach should provide hints (nudge -> technique -> approach) and help debug without just giving the solution.
- Clean hierarchy: The center editor, left explorer, and right utility panels must coexist without causing cognitive overload.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Vietnamese language first, high contrast dark-mode theme, standard keyboard shortcuts for mouse-free navigation.
